What is ShellCoin?
  • ShellCoin (SLCN) is token created on Solana Blockchain technology. The token is created under Token-2022 solana program.
  • It uses consensus mechanism Proof of Stake (PoS). This protocol confirm transactions and create new block through randomly selected validators.
  • Only 10 billion ShellCoin tokens have been minted to preserve their exclusivity; each token represents a finite piece of digital value, ensuring rarity and enduring value for holders.
  • ShellCoin complies with relevant regulations and legal requirements, ensuring a transparent and compliant token sale process.
  • Purpose : ShellCoin aims to become the primary medium of exchange and governance token within the Solana ecosystem. With a clear intention, strong tokenomics, and a pious objective, ShellCoin is poised to drive innovation and growth in the decentralized finance (DeFi) space on Solana.
